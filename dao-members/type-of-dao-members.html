Token Holders & Community Members

  1. Low effort
  2. Advocacy
  3. Varied value

Contributors

  1. Responsible for tasks
  2. Receive bounties based on contributions
  3. Medium to high value

Core Team

  1. Long-term planning
  2. Receive salaries and/or bounties
  3. Highest value

The process of changing DAO member status

E.g. a DAO member going from a token holder or community member to a contributor or core team member depends on factors such as:

  1. Skills
  2. Engagement
  3. Participation
  4. Value
