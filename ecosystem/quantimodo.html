Executive Summary

Our platform collects and aggregates data on symptoms, diet, sleep, exercise, weather, medication, and anything else from dozens of life-tracking apps and devices. Then it analyzes this data to reveal hidden factors exacerbating or improving symptoms of chronic illness.

Value Proposition

Nearly half of Americans suffer from at least one chronic condition, and the number is growing.  The severity of many of these conditions such as arthritis, depression, hypertension, ADHD, anxiety, and diabetes can be influenced by hundreds of factors in daily life.  Human minds do not have the bandwidth or statistical processing power required to account for all these factors, their synergistic relationships, and the time delays before their effects are observed.  So we built a platform that harvests the data from new self-tracking technologies and mines it to help individuals discover which hidden factors are worsening or improving their condition.   

Products/Services

  • Data Collection - The Universal Tracker for Android and Google Chrome enables the user to easily track diet, medication, physical activity, and anything else that can be quantified and automatically upload the data. They also allow users to track their symptom severity and moods at high frequency without interrupting their life.
  • Data Aggregation - The Web Platform is a website that can integrate data from any application or device.   The Connector Framework automatically pulls in data from your life-tracking apps and devices.  The API allows mobile applications to send and access data in the Secure Cloud Storage database.
  • Data Analysis - The Analytics Engine identifies the factors that most influence any given aspect of health and their optimal ranges.
  • Presentation - Aggregated user data is used to determine the factors that most influence any given aspect of health, powering the Search Engine. With the Analytics Dashboard, data streams can be combined and visually analyzed on a timeline to evaluate one’s progress over time and explore the data to find insights. The user is notified of ways to improve their life via personalized real-time mobile notifications.

Target Market

The primary targets include:

  • End-Users - The 613 million people who track a health indicator electronically would like an effortless way to derive actionable insights from their data.
  • Physicians who would like to gain more significant insights into the root causes of their patient’s conditions and identify the optimal treatments and dosages.
  • Health application developers to easily aggregate and analyze their user’s data

Business Model

1. Freemium Consumer Subscription - generates revenue through a subscription freemium model for end-users of the product at $6/month.

2. Developer Platform - It also provides a usage-based subscription platform for health application developers, starting at $0.50/end-user per month.

3. Ad Revenue - By anonymizing and mining the data obtained from these individuals, we can use it to inform the 2 billion people who search for health information online every year via the Search Engine.  Like Google, our revenue is generated by clicks on targeted ads adjacent to these search results.  Google shares 68% of its revenue from each click, producing an average net return of $1.90 per click.

4. Sponsored Apps - Sponsor apps using the platform can be provided to hospitals, insurers, and pharmaceutical companies who wish to improve patient outcomes and potentially gather patient-authorized anonymized patient data. Pricing would follow a subscription model, starting at $2 per end-user per month.
