GDPR Compliance Costs

In the healthcare sector, GDPR compliance implementation costs about $13 million initially and about $1 million per year for maintenance. US HIPAA compliance is far less costly.

Regulatory costs can be diffused through cost-sharing among many companies via a Decentralized Autonomous Organization.

GDPR compliance costs per sector

Another major factor in the cost of GDPR compliance is your industry. A Statista report found that, among FTSE 100 companies, banks spent more than three times as much on GDPR compliance as the next closest sector.
