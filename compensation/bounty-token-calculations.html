Bounty compensation [$] = Multiplier Factor x hourly rate x estimated time x Temp Token Multiplier

 

Determination of factors for Multiplier Factor Formula:

Goal:

1 Importance and 1 Urgency => 0.5 (Multiplier Factor)

5 Importance and 5 Urgency => 2 (Multiplier Factor)

 

Equation subtraction approach:

0.5 = (1)x +y

2 = (25)x +y

 

Solve x:

1.5 = 24x

x = 1.5/24 = 0.0625

 

Solve y:

0.5 = 0.0625 +y

y = 0.4375
