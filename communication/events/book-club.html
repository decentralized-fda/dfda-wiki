Book: Lifespan: Why We Age – and Why We Don't Have To

Author: David Sinclair

When:

Where: CureDAO Discord Office Chat

Moderation:

 

Let's come together to share and discuss Harvard researcher David Sinclair's book "Lifespan: Why We Age – and Why We Don't Have To."

Our goal is to give you a shared experience of reading and discussing the book.

We hope to foster interesting conversations about longevity, health, and biohacking.

This book club is designed as a free-form discussion rather than a panel.

Whether you have read the book or not, we would love to see you there!
