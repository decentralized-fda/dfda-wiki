  2. Drive traffic to increase contributions.
  3. Create the weekly posts in advance for review and planning with scheduling software
  4. Ideally, post in the morning or the late evening of our target groups
  5. AUTHORITY
    1. Post something that creates interest and authority for our customer avatars:
      1. Content pieces that show we know what we’re talking about in terms of our offerings and industry.
        1. Providing RIA (Results-in-Advance).
          1. E.g. Teaching something from our platform
          2. E.g. 1 thing to never do.
          3. E.g. 1 thing to always do
      2. Related to CureDAO's mission statement and objectives
        1. E.g. Why do we do what we do?
      3. Demonstration of walking the walk
        1. For example, talking about something we are currently working on.
          1. For instance, "This is what I was thinking here, I created this because" And so on...
  6. ENGAGEMENT
    1. Contests
    2. Promotions
    3. Surveys
      1. For instance, what does our community want us to change, implement, reduce, or increase? Etc.
    4. Sneak Peeks
    5. Q&A's
    6. Controversial topics
      1. Talk about ideas we are for or against
        1. I.e. trending topics that people are for and against, and then share our take on it.
        2. Ideally, by following these guidelines:
          1. Research the topic
          2. Don’t pick on someone just for the sake of it
          3. Respect fellow people’s opinions
          4. Use current data to back up the points
          5. Mention top social media influencers and request that they share them.
  7. TRUST
    1. Trust is built when two people realize that they are alike.
      1. By having things in common, and sharing similar values.
      2. Showing or demonstrating things we value in life, humanizes our brand and makes it more relatable.
        1. Our core values, passions, interests, etc.
        2. Personal stories
        3. Contributors of the week, month, and year
        4. Testimonials
        5. Transformations, high praise, etc.
      3. Q&A's
  8. GRATITUDE
    1. People are naturally skeptical on social media, but when one experiences gratefulness, it reduces resistance and skepticism.
      1. We are grateful for you, we are grateful for your comments, etc.
      2. Always say thank you and celebrate milestones
        1. E.g. follower count, goals achieved, new initiatives facilitated, etc.
