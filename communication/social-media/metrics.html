METRICS TO TRACK

If we want to achieve the best results possible, tracking is key. How can we achieve the best outcomes without monitoring our initiatives and improving them over time?

  1. Increased contributions:
    1. Within a period of 2 – 3 months, did our content generate an increased amount of contributions? How big of an increase? Etc.
  2. Social media shares, reach, engagement, etc.
  3. Google Analytics – E.g. Traffic, Traffic sources, Session Duration, Pages per visit, and Returning visitors
  4. Use the tool, “Unionmetrics” to track Twitter engagement, “Audiense” to track our ideal audience, and “Talkwalker” to track trending relevant topics to cover
  5. For other relevant platforms, find ideal tracking platforms such as tracking Discord engagement
    1. E.g. use Statbot to track
    2. Review user comments, summarize topics and posts that got the most engagement
    3. Channels that got the most engagement
