1. Prizes that are meaningful to the audience.
  2. Give them something that represents us.
  3. Giveaway experiences, not just goods and services.
  4. E.g. event tickets to healthy longevity, biohacking, health data, and web 3 conferences.
  5. At least prize for first, second, and third place.
    1. Ideally also an incentive for participating
    2. People want to feel like they have a chance of winning, and if you only have a grand prize, then they might not play along.
  6. Give them more entries to win the contest based on how much they give us in exchange.
    1. For an email address, they get one entry.
    2. If they get a person to join our Discord, they get 1 more entry.
    3. For a share, they might get 3 more entries.
    4. If they get a new contributor on board, they get 6 more entries
    5. If they get another person to join the contest, they get 1 more entry, etc.
  7. Ideally, we run it for about a month.
  8. Make it a big deal when we announce the winners and use this occasion as another event that generates noise and traffic.
