Design Lab

  • CureDAO is helping create personalized precision medicine of the future by establishing an open-source platform for health data
  • Design work is essential for any DAO. Naturally, we want to ensure that CureDAO's design is perfectly aligned with our mission and vision

 

Benefits

A share of 10% of the total outstanding CureDAO tokens, proportional to your hourly rate and number of hours invested.

Based on comparable health DAO launches, this would likely lead to far greater compensation than your standard rate.

Other benefits include:

  • Partake in governance decisions
  • Flexible remote work hours

 

Responsibilities

  • The Design Lab is responsible for the design work of CureDAO's community and operations
  • The role includes collaborating with lab members and consultants on a wide variety of design tasks
  • The lab monitors the characteristics of our community, optimizes the design, and aligns it with CureDAO's mission and vision

 

Qualifications

  • Strong willingness to learn and grow
  • Great problem solver
  • Passionate about our vision
  • Good design skills
